Android & iOS AR Apps
  • Our Augmented Reality solutions are robust, scalable, and secure, ensuring compatibility with the latest operating systems. Users can seamlessly access these apps across various devices, offering them engaging, interactive, and personalized experiences.
Integration of AR into e-commerce
  • To drive sales, enhance branding, and replicate in-store experiences, our AR developers create innovative shopping experiences for e-commerce augmented apps.
Development of AR game apps
  • Our specialized AR game developers create immersive gaming experiences using Unity, Corona, and Unreal Engine tools. Let us help you deliver an unforgettable experience to your customers.
AR based on location
  • By leveraging GPS, accelerometer, and compass technologies, location-based AR apps can accurately pinpoint user positions. Enhance the success of your augmented apps by targeting and engaging customers based on their real-time location.
AR based on projections
  • We create projection-based AR applications that project virtual images onto real-world objects, seamlessly integrating with the physical environment. Enhance your augmented apps with this cutting-edge technology to create deeper connections with your customers.
A recognition-based AR system
  • We utilize marker-based Augmented Reality to replace visual markers with corresponding objects, triggered by QR codes. With the support of the Wikitude library, these apps can recognize objects and display them with 3D imagery.
A superimposition-based AR system
  • Superimposition-based AR uses object recognition technology to replace real-world objects or parts with augmented views. Enhance your customer experience by investing in these innovative apps.
