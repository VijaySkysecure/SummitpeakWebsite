FAQ

1. What is contract staffing? ▼
Contract staffing typically involves hiring candidates on a temporary or contractual basis as per changing demands of the business in both the long term and short term.
2. How do contract staffing agencies work? ▼
A contract staffing agency is a recruiting firm that acts as an intermediary whose responsibility is to connect businesses to potential candidates looking for temporary or contract work. A contract staffing agency recruits and manages workers for clients, taking care of sourcing, onboarding, payroll and administration.
3. How to find a good contract staffing agency? ▼
Companies that prefer to outsource the employment search and screening process must consider the reputation, policies, practices and costs of employment agencies. The two things to think about when finding a good temp agency are firstly, to look for an agency that specializes in your field, is well-known and has good contacts in the industry, and secondly, to decide and choose whether you want to go for a larger agency or a smaller one depending on your recruitment needs.
4. Why contract staffing services? ▼
If you are an organisation looking to hire employees on a short-term basis to help on a specific project or as an addition to the team due to surge in workload, then contract staffing services is the perfect solution for you as you are allowed to hire them on a contract basis and not permanently eliminating the overhead costs of hiring a permanent employee.
5. What are the key roles of a contract staffing agency? ▼
The key roles of a contract staffing agency include sourcing and recruiting candidates, matching candidates with job openings, and screening and evaluating candidates.
